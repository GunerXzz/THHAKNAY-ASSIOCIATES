Corporate Law

Our firm provides comprehensive counsel on all aspects of corporate law, from entity formation and governance to complex mergers and acquisitions. We guide our clients through business structuring, ensuring full compliance and strategic advantage in the Cambodian market.

We act as trusted advisors for day-to-day operations and major transactions alike, prioritizing your business's long-term success.

Litigation

When disputes arise, you need a strong and strategic advocate. Our litigation team has a formidable reputation for representing clients in complex commercial, civil, and regulatory matters. We meticulously prepare every case, from pre-litigation negotiation to courtroom representation.

We focus on achieving the most favorable outcome, whether through strategic settlement or assertive trial advocacy.

Compliance

Navigating Cambodia's regulatory landscape is a critical challenge. Our compliance team provides expert guidance to ensure your business operates with full integrity and avoids costly penalties. We assist with licensing, reporting, and developing robust internal compliance programs.

We are your partners in maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct.

Contract Law

Clear, enforceable contracts are the bedrock of any successful business. Our attorneys are meticulous in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating commercial agreements. We handle everything from shareholder and partnership agreements to distribution and licensing contracts.

Our focus is on protecting your interests, minimizing risk, and ensuring your agreements are perfectly aligned with your business goals.

Real Estate

We provide comprehensive legal services for all aspects of real estate law in Cambodia. This includes acquisitions and dispositions, development, leasing, financing, and property dispute resolution. We guide our clients through complex zoning, titling, and regulatory hurdles.

We ensure your property transactions are secure, compliant, and structured for success.

Employment

Managing labor relations is essential for a stable and productive workforce. Our employment law team advises on workplace policies, employment contracts, labor disputes, and compliance with all relevant Cambodian labor laws.

We help employers navigate employee rights, disciplinary actions, and terminations, protecting your business from potential disputes.
