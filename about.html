WHO WE ARE

Your Dedicated Legal Partners

At THHAKNAY & ASSOCIATES, we are committed to delivering exceptional legal services tailored to the unique needs of our clients. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the law, we provide comprehensive solutions across a broad range of practice areas. These include business law, litigation, real estate, family law, intellectual property, taxation, bankruptcy and insolvency, licensing, alternative dispute resolution, and more.

Our team of dedicated attorneys combines legal expertise with a client-focused approach, ensuring personalized attention and strategic guidance at every step. We have extensive experience examining and negotiating a wide range of contracts and agreements—including agency and distribution agreements, partnership agreements, shareholder agreements, and other business contracts. We pride ourselves on our integrity, professionalism, and unwavering commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes.

OUR MISSION

Why we exist as a firm?

Our mission is to provide outstanding legal services that deliver the best possible client outcomes. We specialize in coordinating and representing our clients with exceptional service and extrajudicial solutions. Furthermore, our dedicated research team persistently updates its legal knowledge through continuous training, research, and engagement with governmental and non-governmental institutions.

OUR VISION

Our Vision & Values

We are more than just lawyers; we are your strategic partners. Our vision is built on these core values:

  • To be our clients’ most trusted advisors.
  • To uphold the strictest standards of professional secrecy and client confidentiality.
  • To be a great place to work, fostering a collaborative and supportive environment.
  • To be a successful and profitable firm, ensuring long-term stability and growth.
  • To maintain an outstanding reputation within the legal and other professions.
  • To restore humanity and the act of kindness in our practice and community.
